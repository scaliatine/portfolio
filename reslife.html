Texas State Housing and Residential Life

Keys to Success

The Department of Housing and Residential Life at Texas State (DHRL) provides welcoming inclusive living communities for a diverse student population to foster academic success, campus engagement, and personal development.

Unpacking the Details

When Texas State University went through a website redesign, it was the perfect opportunity for DHRL to completely reapproach their website User Experience. At the time there were multiple pages with duplicate content, pages that required manual updates at a very frequent rate and an unclear user flow.

Questions

  • How might we introduce the housing process to parents?
  • How might we cater to different demographics?
  • How might we plan an experience for visitors?
  • How might we display content that doesn't require constant updating?

Mapping the Path

Part of the design process was to take a complete inventory of all the different web pages within the structure of the site. Then all the copy was assessed and rewritten with a clear tone of voice. This process reduced the page count by 25%.
