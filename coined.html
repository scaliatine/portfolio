Coined is a creative blog that takes a critical look at the thought and development that goes behind the products, businesses and logos published in video games, movies and tv shows.

Player Guides

Utilizing The Sprint method, a five-day process that helps solve design problems quickly by determining the problem, answering critical questions, protoyping and testing ideas with final users, a clear path was identified resulting in the final outcome.

Goals

The goal is to create a site that mutliple writers could easily submit content to encourage visitors to think critically in regards to design within video game and movies.

Questions

  • How might we organize different stories and articles effectively?
  • How might we create a system that allows sumbissions to be formated without coding?
  • How might we create a site that is easy to dissiminate informtion in social platforms?

Wireframes

Unbranded and then fully-branded wireframes were tested to determine the success of both the UX/UI of the responsive site.

Achievement Unlocked

Final Outcome

Created as a Wordpress site, allowing writers to publish articles without dealing with HTML / CSS formating, the site functions to discuss the world of fictional branding across video games, movies and TV shows.
