We A.R.e Bauhaus

Form

Designers connected to the Bauhaus celebrated the ordinary that may have been previously ignored. In celebration of the Bauhaus 100 year anniversary, the exhibit would consist of large letters, visible only through using the Microsoft HoloLens, on the walls in the Bauhaus Master Houses in Dessau representing each designer using Augmented Reality.

Follows

We ARe Bauhaus will turn what is considered ordinary into a focal point—much like the artists in the exhibit.
