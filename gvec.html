Guadalupe Valley Electric Cooperative

Watts the Deal

GVEC is a cooperative with two sides: membership in the not-for-profit electric portion of their business which features exclusive member benefits, and additional services beyond the meter extended to both members and the general public. Their goal is to be more than just a power company.

Taking the Steps

Goal

The goal is to redesign the electric bill served to over 40,000 electric cooperative members and redesign the cooperative's website which features many of the services they offer.

Questions

  • How might we increase transparency for how members are being charged?
  • How might we encourage members to participate in the other services provided?
  • How can we show the impact within the community GVEC makes?

Previous Designs

Lighting the Way

Final Outcome

A responsive website, with a clear voice, showcases the multitude of services GVEC offers its members, allowing for quick access to their needs. This proposal site was introduced in the early stages of a complete redesign and eventually changed beyond this concept.
