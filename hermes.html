Hermes Travel Site

Pack Your Bags

Utilizing The Sprint method, a five-day process that helps solve design problems quickly by determining the problem, answering critical questions, protoyping and testing ideas with final users, a clear path was identified resulting in the final outcome.

Goals

Create a responsive website for a new piece of technology, specifically Virtual Reality, explaining the benefits, purpose and possible uses.

Questions

  • How might we create a travel experience, all within a digital platform?
  • How might we encourage users to participate if they don't have a VR device?
  • How might we showcase specific destinations to promote travel to those locations?

Sketches

Sketches were created and then user tested helping create the final layout to move into wireframing.

Wireframes

Unbranded and then fully-branded wireframes were tested to determine the success of both the UX/UI of the responsive site.

Destination Reached

Final Outcome

A responsive website, with a clear voice, showcases the many locations that users can visit, all from the safety of their home.
